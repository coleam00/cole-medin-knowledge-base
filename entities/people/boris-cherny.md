# Boris Cherny

Boris Cherny is a creator and maintainer of [Claude Code](../tools/claude-code.md), and in this catalog he is the practitioner whose public statements Cole uses to mark where [The Evolution of AI Coding](../../concepts/the-evolution-of-ai-coding.md) is heading. The defining quote is his description of how he now works: "Boris, the creator of Claude Code, said... I don't prompt Claude anymore. I write loops and the loops do the work. My job is to write loops" ([0:01:40]). That single line captures the move past hand-holding prompts toward orchestrated, self-running agent loops, and Cole underscores the scale it enables: "Boris Cherny says that there are days he manages tens of thousands of AI agents at once" ([0:06:10]).

His influence is large enough that his words get over-read, and Cole is careful to correct the record. When a podcast remark was taken to mean software engineering was finished, "he literally clarified later on X that software engineering is still very important" ([0:03:28]), adding the fuller framing: "Someone has to prompt the clouds. Talk to the customers... Engineering is changing and great engineers are more important than ever" ([0:03:44]). That nuance is why Cole ties Boris to [Human in the Loop](../../concepts/human-in-the-loop.md), [Intent Engineering](../../concepts/intent-engineering.md), and [Validation](../../concepts/validation.md) rather than to pure autonomy. Cole also treats Boris as a bellwether whose takes carry weight and therefore warrant scrutiny: "Boris Cherny, he's one of the maintainers of Claude Code. A lot of people take his tweets as gospel" ([0:07:19]).

## Realizes

- [The PIV Loop](../../concepts/the-piv-loop.md) - Cole's core agentic-coding cycle of Plan, Implement, Validate that structures every unit of AI-driven work.
- [Intent Engineering](../../concepts/intent-engineering.md) - Shifting the engineer's job from writing code to precisely expressing intent that an AI agent implements.
- [Human in the Loop](../../concepts/human-in-the-loop.md) - Keeping a human as the approver and steerer of agentic work rather than fully automating, so AI augments engineers instead of replacing them.
- [Validation](../../concepts/validation.md) - Building explicit checks into agentic workflows so the AI verifies its own output against real success criteria before moving on.
