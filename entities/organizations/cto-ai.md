# CTO.ai

CTO.ai is the company founded by [Lior Weinstein](../people/lior-weinstein.md), Cole's collaborator on the AI transformation side of his agentic-coding work. Cole introduces the partnership in terms of what Lior teaches rather than the product: in the workshop tie-in he says "Leor is going to teach us how to restructure our entire organization for AI." Where Cole's own material focuses on the hands-on craft of building with [Claude Code](../tools/claude-code.md) and coding agents, CTO.ai's angle is the organizational layer, how a company reshapes its teams, roles, and processes so that AI adoption actually sticks.

That framing places CTO.ai adjacent to Cole's methodology rather than inside it. His technical throughline is [context engineering](../../concepts/context-engineering.md): managing what an agent knows at any moment by fighting [context rot](../../concepts/context-rot.md), performing deliberate [context resets](../../concepts/context-reset.md), and treating [git as long-term memory](../../concepts/git-as-long-term-memory.md) so knowledge survives across sessions. CTO.ai's contribution complements this from the top down, pairing the individual-engineer practices Cole demonstrates with the higher-level restructuring an organization needs to run them at scale, including disciplined [planning with AI](../../concepts/planning-with-ai.md) and a named methodology, the [WHISK framework](../../concepts/the-whisk-framework.md).

The org surfaces once in the catalog, as a partner voice inside the 2,000-hours Claude Code video, so it functions here as a pointer to the people-and-process dimension of AI adoption rather than a standalone tool review. It sits naturally alongside Cole's own community, [Dynamous](./dynamous.md), which runs the enterprise-training and workshop track for the same audience.
