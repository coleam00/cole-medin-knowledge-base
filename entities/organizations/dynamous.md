# Dynamous

Dynamous (dynamous.ai) is the paid community Cole founded for early AI adopters, and the place where most of his deeper teaching happens beyond YouTube. He describes it plainly: "check out dynamis.ai. It's my community for other early AI adopters just like yourself. And as a part of it, I'm building out a full course" ([0:21:05]). It is the successor to his earlier [oTTomator](./ottomator.md) community, now centered on the **Agentic Coding course** (formerly the AI Agent Mastery course), which he calls "the best resource you'll find on the internet for learning how to build reliable and repeatable workflows for AI coding" ([0:26:25]).

Two things make Dynamous load-bearing for this catalog. First, the community is where Cole prototypes ideas before they reach the channel, running weekly half-day workshops, "I'm running a special 4-hour workshop in the Dynamis community this Friday" ([0:09:15]), on topics from [Second Brain](../../concepts/second-brain.md) builds to [RAG](../../concepts/rag.md) pipelines. Second, it hosts the **Context Engineering Hub**, a collaboratively built library: "the context engineering hub. This is going to be basically a gold mine of resources for PRP templates and slash commands and global rules and cloud hooks and sub agents that everyone is contributing together" ([0:39:00]). That hub is the community engine behind [Context Engineering](../../concepts/context-engineering.md), [Global Rules](../../concepts/global-rules.md), and the [PRP Framework](../../concepts/prp-framework.md).

The course codifies the same disciplines the videos preach: [Planning with AI](../../concepts/planning-with-ai.md), [PRD-First Development](../../concepts/prd-first-development.md), [Validation](../../concepts/validation.md), and keeping a [Human in the Loop](../../concepts/human-in-the-loop.md) on each build. Dynamous is also where [Archon](../tools/archon.md) took shape: "the past few months, me and a few other guys from the Dynamis community have been shaping this new vision and platform for Archon" ([0:00:15]).
