# AI Agent Business Niches

An AI agent business niche is a concrete, monetizable use case where a productized agent solves a repeatable problem for a defined market, rather than a general-purpose assistant chasing everything at once. The most reliable niche is lead generation: "one use case though that is tried and true and really necessary for any business is a lead generation agent" ([0:00:00]). Such an agent "sits on your website and runs the front end of your business while you sleep ... lead qualification answering any questions suggesting products or resources and collecting contact information for follow-ups" ([0:00:14]). It is durable because every business needs pipeline, and the agent runs around the clock.

A second strategy is to target an underserved but high-value platform. Cole's pick is building agents on top of a marketing-agency CRM: "this Niche is building AI agents for an incredible platform called go high level go high level is essentially a CRM for marketing agencies" ([0:00:16]). The platform is fertile because "they have an incredible API in an app Marketplace" ([0:02:43]), so agents can plug straight into existing agency workflows and be sold through a distribution channel. Digital marketing rewards exactly what agents are good at: "the name of the game for digital marketing speed volume and personalization and guess what is really good at doing this if you do it right that's right it's ai" ([0:03:27]).

A third angle is reusable, open-source agents as a community and portfolio. The Live Agent Studio is "an incredible community-driven platform ... filled with a bunch of cuttingedge agents that you can try yourself and they're all open source so you can download them and learn how to implement them" ([0:01:21]). Publishing a working agent both demonstrates capability and seeds reuse.
